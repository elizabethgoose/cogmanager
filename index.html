HOUSE MALT SYRUP
Baker's target: ~2% of flour weight in dough. Also add 1–2 tbsp per gallon to boiling water for crust color and that unmistakable bagel flavor. Your house-reduced brewery malt is the real deal — treat it accordingly.

OKC humidity note: Ciabatta at 80% hydration is already on the edge. On dry days (humidity <45%), don't be tempted to add more water — the flour needs time to absorb. On humid days (>70%), you may need to extend bulk ferment by 15–20 min as the dough will be slightly slacker. Let the dough tell you, not the clock.
